Game Projects

2D PLATFORMER SHOOTER GAME

Immerse yourself in an adrenaline-pumping 2D action platformer where agility and precision are your greatest allies. As the hero, your mission is to traverse perilous levels teeming with danger, using your wits and weaponry to overcome a relentless wave of enemies. Navigate intricate platforming challenges, uncover hidden paths, and collect power-ups to enhance your abilities. The ultimate goal? Face off against a formidable boss in an epic showdown that will test your skills to the limit. With fast-paced combat, engaging level design, and a captivating retro aesthetic, this game delivers an exhilarating experience for players seeking action-packed thrills and satisfying victories.

3D HORROR GAME

Step into the chilling world of a 3D horror experience where survival hinges on your courage and cunning. Trapped inside a dark and foreboding building, your only chance of escape is to locate three hidden keys to unlock the gate to freedom. But you re not alone. A relentless and terrifying monster stalks your every move, forcing you to stay vigilant and strategic. Armed with just a flashlight, you can temporarily repel the creature, buying precious seconds to search for the keys and plan your next move. With eerie atmospherics, tense gameplay, and heart-pounding encounters, this game delivers a gripping survival horror adventure that keeps you on the edge of your seat until the very end.

3D SURVIVAL GAME

In this collaborative project, my primary focus was on procedural generation and optimization to enhance both gameplay and performance. I developed the system responsible for procedurally generating the terrain and desert buildings, creating a dynamic and immersive environment. Additionally, I implemented procedural generation for the interior layouts of buildings, ensuring varied and engaging exploration experiences for players. To support smooth gameplay, I worked on optimizing the terrain rendering, significantly reducing performance bottlenecks and delivering a seamless and visually appealing experience.
